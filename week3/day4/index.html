Week 3 - Day 4

August 4th

You’ll be ending your week with a fresh Battleroom :bomb: and a prior project. And of course an end of week survey.

Activity Name Description of activity
Bonding Game
9:00 AM		 Your peers are your brothers, you’ll be playing a bonding game every morning to have fun, get to know each other, and have a nice start of the day.
Battleroom!
9:30 AM		 This week on Battleroom, six brand new challenges for you to attempt! Don’t forget the three pillars of ASC!
Pick a prior project
10:45 AM		 Your task today is to pick a project you’ve already done, and revamp it / add new features to it using all the ne skills you’ve acquired since doing it originally.
Pick a prior project Demo
3:15 PM		 Time to demo your projects and get some sponsors!
End of week survey
3:45 PM		 A survey asking for feedback on the week.
Office Hours
4:00 PM		 At the end of each day there’s time for you to work with your instructors to catchup on anything you had trouble with or wanted more practice with. Think of it as a long wiggle room, in which you the students choose what to work on. While it is optional for students, we strongly encourage you to use this time to your advantage.
